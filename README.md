### Description:

Math calculator with basic arithmetic operations without eval

`npm run calc`

Then you can calc simple math operations

Examples:

```
> 10 + 10
Result: 20
> 10 + 10 * 20 - 30
Result: 180
> 19 + -10
Result: 9
```

Also you can calc math operations with priority and brackets

"\*": multiply,
"/": divide,
"^": power,
"!": factorial,
"\*\*": square,

Examples:

```
> (1 + 2) ^ (5 - 3 * 1)
Result: 10
> (1 + ((2 !) + 5) * 2) - (2 ** + 0 !)
Result: 10
```
